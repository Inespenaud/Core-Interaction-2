This website explores the multiple styles of AI (artificial intelligence) generated art.

This art wass created on a website called Wombo where you select categories and art gerneated by AI from it.

For this particular project we typed in art and chose the category to create the art work.

This was a collaborative process, as there were two people involved in creating this collection of work.

The first person, the creator of the collection was Hannah, she created 50 AI art pieces which she filtered in their style.

Later on she passed the collection to me Ines, and I continued to create 50 more AI generated art pieces, sorting them into categories which became the ones used on the website.

This website aims to demonstrate a selection of multiple AI styles as a demonstration of the vastness that Artificial Intelligence within the art world.
